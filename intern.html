About

We are looking for highly motivated and bright students to continue building the foundation of our summer intern program. Guided by our team of software professionals, and previous Interns, you will apply cutting edge software development tools and practices to a real software product that you will build from the ground up. Some of the technologies considered for the project are Vue/Angular/React, Node/Express, and Bootstrap/Google Material Design. Throughout the program, Interns will use many software engineering best practices such as agile/scrum, pair programming, automated testing, and test-driven development. If you are eager to hone the skills learned at Virginia Tech and want to work on a real-world problem, from concept to delivery, while working for an established company of software professionals, then please consider applying for a summer internship with Case Consulting!

What is a government contractor?

  • A government contractor is someone who is contracted out to work directly for the government or for a prime contractor that owns a government contract. A contractor works at a government or prime facility along with contractors from other companies. At Case Consulting, it is our goal to have two or more teammates on the same contract working together.

What is the value of a security clearance?

  • A security clearance opens the door to an exciting world of opportunity that not everyone qualifies for. If you make the cut, you’ll have access to mission sensitive jobs with intriguing problems that need to be solved. Not to mention, there’s a high demand for software engineers with a security clearance which provides added job security.

Why is there a 1 year commitment?

  • A commitment to Case Consulting is necessary to submit you for a U.S. Government security clearance. Also, as a company we take our investment into our college hires very seriously. We pour a lot of time, energy, and resources into this program so that it is not an average internship experience, but rather true development of the skills required to succeed as a software engineer in the real world.

    To make this decision easier, in addition to the offer for the summer internship, you will also receive an offer for full time employment with Case Consulting, including your minimum starting salary. We understand that this is a big commitment, but we believe you will love our culture as much as we do and will want to stay well past any specified commitment time period.

Where is the internship located?

  • The internship in located at our shared office space in Reston, VA.

Once full time where would I be working?

  • Case Consulting has quite a few projects to place college new hires on when they start full time. Our goal is to find one that best fits your skill-sets and to place you on a team with other Case Consulting employees. Most of Case Consulting’s work is located in the Northern Virginia area to include Tyson’s, McLean, Bethesda, Reston, Herndon, and Chantilly.

Is there any relocation or housing compensation for the internship?

  • Case Consulting doesn't offer relocation or housing compensation for the internship program but would work with our network to help find housing for the summer.

What technologies will I be working with during the internship?

  • It is our objective to have interns working with the most current technologies and industry best practices. Tech changes fast and we work to keep you as current as possible. Some of the technologies that have been used in past years are JavaScript, Node/Express, Angular, Vue, Amazon Web Services and Bootstrap.
